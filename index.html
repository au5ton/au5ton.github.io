Who I Am & What I Do

My name is Austin Jackson. I am a 15 year old Java programmer and web developer from Houston, Texas. I like Daft Punk, spending time with my girlfriend, and good Mexican food. I revere Apple and those who think like them — a passion for the user experience. I like my work to be well-designed, and to look and feel exquisite. I'm a perfectionist and problem solver fueled by rigid inspiration, and a big supporter of open-source software.

I specialize in Java programming — a platform-independent desktop programming langauge — and do HTML, JavaScript, and CSS projects on the side.

My Previous Work

My projects are often created out of one or more of these two things:
1) Inspiration to create my own work or to recreate the work of others,
2) Solve my own or someone else's problems.
That being said, not all of my work may be useful for everyone, but useful for people who had a similar problem that I did, or that want to learn from my mistakes.

Check out my Github profile to see some of my open-source projects, including this very website.
