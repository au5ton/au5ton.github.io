Who I Am & What I Do

My name is Austin Jackson. I am a 15 year old Java programmer and web developer from Houston, Texas. I like Daft Punk, spending time with my girlfriend, and good Mexican food. I revere Apple and those who think like them — a passion for the user experience. I like my work to be well-designed, and to look and feel exquisite. I'm a perfectionist fueled by rigid inspiration, and a big supporter of open-source software.

I specialize in Java programming — a platform-independent desktop programming langauge — and do HTML, JavaScript, and CSS projects on the side.
